# Blinded

Attacks made by the enemy can be reduced by spending half the Defense Slots (rounded down).

# Bloodied

If they take any additional Wound, they die. (Narrative finality flag)

# Burning

At the end of their round, they suffer a Wound.

# Charmed

They cannot target the charmer. The charmer can spend 2 Actions to issue a simple, non-self-destructive command. Ends if the charmer or allies damage them.

# Confused

At the start of their turn, they roll 1d6: 1–3 = Act erratically (you control) 4–6 = Act normally. Cannot use Interruptions.

# Dazed

Their AP pool is reduced by one. Cannot use Mythic Actions.

# Frightened

Cannot move toward or target the source of fear. While the source is visible, attacks against them gain a Boon.

# Frozen

They cannot move. All physical attacks against you deal half the damage rolled, again.

# Knocked Down

They are Prone. Melee attackers gain a Boon against them. Spend 1 Action to stand.

# Poisoned

At the start of their turn, they loses 1 Action. At the end of their turn, if untreated, suffer 1 Wound.

# Rooted

Their Speed becomes 0. Attacks against them gain a Boon.

# Silenced

They cannot speak or cast spells unless they Subtle Cast or use similar abilities.

# Slowed

Movement is halved.

# Stunned

They cannot act, move, or react. Melee attacks against them have a Boon.

# Unseen

While undetected, they cannot be targeted.

# Weakened

Their attacks deal half damage.
